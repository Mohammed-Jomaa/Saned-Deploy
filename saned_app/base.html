Saned is a community-driven web platform designed to streamline and organize the donation and aid process in Palestine. It connects families in need, donors, and local NGOs or mosques through a transparent and secure digital system that ensures dignity and accountability. The platform enables beneficiaries to submit requests privately, allows NGOs to verify and manage aid distribution, and provides donors with real-time visibility and confidence in the impact of their contributions. Each user role has a tailored dashboard to provide a seamless and role-specific experience.

Want to start your own campaign?

Through this platform, you can help people in need or provide support in emergencies. You can also direct gifts for special occasions, such as your birthday or wedding, by requesting donations in lieu of gifts and allocating them to support children's education. You can also choose the area you wish to support, such as water, sanitation, and hygiene in schools, early childhood development, youth empowerment programs, girls' education, teacher training, and other areas. Additionally, when you register on the platform, you can easily track contributions directed to your campaign.
